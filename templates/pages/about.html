First and foremost, thanks for visiting my Portfolio. The idea was to create something that stands out amongst other developers.

The portfolio should be a reflection of the developer's personality and skills. I've designed this to be simple, clean, and easy to navigate (with Discord left tab inspiration).

The stack I used for this website is: Go HTMX TailwindCSS MySQL

Why Go?

  • • High level with low level performance
  • • Good for upcoming programming technology trends

HTMX?

  • • Contains lightweight JS (might research more on this)

Tailwind?

  • • For more personal touch compared to Bootstrap

MySQL?

  • • Good for beginners and small projects
